Services We Provide

Building Plan

Building Plan is a set of documents and drawings used by architects and builders to construct a building. It outlines the design, materials, and layout of the building, as well as detailing any specific requirements for its construction. Building Plans can also include details on safety and building codes, energy efficiency, and other regulations. Building Plans are essential for any construction project and must be approved by local authorities before construction can begin.

Estimation

Estimation is the process of estimating the value or size of something. It involves making educated guesses based on available information and using a variety of techniques to arrive at a reasonable estimate. We provide a comprehensive range of estimation services, from detailed cost estimates to risk analysis and forecasting. Our experienced professionals are uniquely qualified to provide accurate, timely, and cost-effective estimates that meet your needs.

Buildings

Contract works in buildings involve the construction, alteration, maintenance, repair or improvement of a building or structure. This includes the labour, materials and services required to complete the job. Contract works may involve the installation of plumbing, electrical wiring, painting, flooring, tiling or other related works. Contractors may also provide advice and assistance with the planning of the project and the overall design of the building.
